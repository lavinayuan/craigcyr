CRCpicture Productions

CRCpicture Productions Inc. is a Canadian production entity associated with film, stunt, and acting work, notably in projects like "Dual Suspects" and "Murder in Paradise". It operates within the Canadian independent film and television landscape and focuses on event production, lighting, and sound, noted for services in local entertainment.

Craig Cyr, Owner and CEO, has been in the film industry since the early 90's. He is an accomplished Actor, Stunt Performer/ Coordinator as well as Fight Choreographer and Professional Fighter. He is now producing and directing short films as well as full length features. Craig has the contacts, knowledge and skills on all aspects of film making.

At CRCpicture Productions our team is dedicated to bringing the absolute best in performance and budget to any project.
